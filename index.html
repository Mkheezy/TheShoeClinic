Services

Colour Restoration

In the life time of your shoe, the colour starts to fade. We use a specialised formula to bring your shoe back to life, restoring it's colour and extending the shoe's life.

Sole Restoration

With some shoes, the sole starts to change colour. This phenomenon is common in white shoes, where the sole turns yellow as a result of oxidation. With our sole restoration we restore the sole to it's pearly white state.
